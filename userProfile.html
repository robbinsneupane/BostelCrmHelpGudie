Managing Profile

This is the section where you can oversee and manage your profile. The primary sections of this feature include:

  • User Profile Tab
  • Password Tab
  • Advanced Tab

Once you log in to the system, you'll have access to numerous configuration options. To view or modify your preferences, simply click on your user name located on the top right of the navigation menu.

User Profile Tab

Upon clicking to access your preferences, you'll be directed to the 'User Profile' tab, which provides a summary of your credentials, such as Username, First Name, Last Name, Title, and more. This tab enables you to edit, update, and even add a profile picture to your information.

Password Tab

Upon clicking to access your preferences, you'll be directed to the 'User Profile' tab, which provides a summary of your credentials, such as Username, First Name, Last Name, Title, and more. This tab enables you to edit, update, and even add a profile picture to your information.

In the event that you've forgotten your password and cannot log in, you can utilize the password recovery feature explained in the Getting Started section of this User Guide.

Advanced Tab

The Advanced tab offers you the capability to modify general preferences, such as notification settings, time zone, and display formats for date, time, and name.
