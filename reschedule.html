Rescheduling a Call

To reschedule a Call, you can click the 'Reschedule' button under the Actions tab on the Detail View of a Call which has been defined as Outbound and Planned.

Tracking History

After you have saved the changes to the call's date and time, it will be automatically rescheduled accordingly. To see the history of all rescheduled calls, go to the Detail View of the call and click on the "Reschedule" tab.
