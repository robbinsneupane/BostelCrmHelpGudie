Invoice

Creating an Invoice

The process of creating an Invoice record closely resembles that of creating a Quote record. To initiate the creation of an Invoice, you can either select 'Create Invoice' from the actions bar within the Invoices module, or click the 'Add' button located at the bottom. The first panel of the creation process allows you to specify crucial details about the Invoice, such as the Status and Due Date.

The second panel focuses on the recipient of the Invoice from the Contacts module. Upon selecting the appropriate Contact, the Billing Address is automatically retrieved and populated into the relevant fields of the Invoice record. In the event that the Shipping Address matches the Billing Address, clicking the "Same As Billing Address" button will efficiently duplicate the information into the relevant Shipping Address fields.

Line Items with Groups

Users have the ability to include Groups and Line Items in their Invoices via this module. The process is identical to that of creating Groups and Line Items for Quotes.

Please refer to the Quotes section for details on how to create Groups and Line Items.

Sending Invoices

To generate output for an Invoice, you can select one of the following three buttons from the Invoice Detail View. These three methods are:

  • Print as PDF

    Allows you to select a template and download or save a PDF of the Invoice.

  • Email PDF

    Allows you to select a template and then directs you to the email client 'Compose' screen. The Invoice PDF will be attached to the email and the email will be addressed to the related Contact of the Invoice. This allows you to fill out the email body.

  • Email Invoice

    This directs you to the email client 'Compose' screen. The email will be addressed to the related Contact of the Invoice. There will be no attachment and the Invoice will be displayed within the body of the email.
